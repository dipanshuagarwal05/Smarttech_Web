Digital Project Overview

Digital Grampanchayat

The Digital Grampanchayat project is designed to support the digital transformation of village panchayats. This page clearly presents the core service and goals of the project.

Download Project PDF

Digital Grampanchayat Project

Under Digital India, the idea of a digital village is very useful for building modern gram panchayats through technology. While following the Maharashtra government's information and technology policy, this project helps deliver gram panchayat information and scheme details to the public, including the weaker and last sections of society.

Main Objectives

Under Digital Grampanchayat, your gram panchayat and villagers can access the following services.

Facilities Available in This App

* Gram Panchayat mobile app

* Village announcements on mobile

* All tax payments online (house tax, water tax, street light)

* Information about village development works

* Information about government schemes

* Facility to buy and sell goods

* Job updates

* Information about all state and central government schemes

* Information about educational, social, and health institutions

* Information about government programs

* Updates on new employment opportunities

* Proper information about energy, agriculture, education, and health

* Easy promotion for different goods and services

* Information about village industries

* India is going digital, make your village digital

* Village history and basic information about the village.

* Officials: complete details of panchayat officials and staff.

* Services and schemes: information about gram panchayat, state, and central government schemes.

* Market rates: farmers receive updated market rates every day.

* E-dawandi: an important menu for panchayat notices and other updates.

* News: major world updates and newspaper news.

* Development works: information about work happening in the village.

* Marketplace: an online option to advertise and sell products or items.

* Health: information about village health facilities.

* School: information about village schools and education services.

* Jobs: various employment opportunities for educated youth.

* Important contacts: all important village contact numbers in one place.

* Industry: information about different industries and businesses.

* Developmentpedia: important information about energy, education, and agriculture policies.
